How to play

Goal: be the first player with no cards left.

  • On your turn, play one card on the discard pile.
  • Your card must match the top card by suit or by rank.
  • If you cannot play, draw a card from the deck.
  • Some cards have special effects, like skipping, reversing, or making someone draw cards.

How to play

Goal: be the first player with no cards left.

  • Match the top card by suit or rank.
  • Draw from the deck if you cannot play.
  • Jack lets you choose a suit. Joker can always be played.
  • 2 makes the next player draw two cards. Jokers add five cards.
  • 7 and King give you another turn. 8 skips. Ace reverses. 10 rotates hands.
